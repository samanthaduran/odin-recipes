Noods

Description

Noodles, more commonly known as "noods", are a popular stable in the Duran-Hickey household. Featuring Sainsbury's (and only Sainsbury's) fresh egg noodles, soy sauce, brown sugar, and a few other ingredients, they make for great leftovers

Noodles take about 30 minutes to prepare from start to finish, and you can add your favorite protein or substitute some extra vegetables if you wish.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages Sainsbury's Fresh Egg Noodles
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion
  • 250 g mushrooms
  • 100 g frozen peas
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Chop onion, mushroom and garlic into chunks
  2. Heat up 1 Tsbp olive oil in large pan
  3. Add onion, mushroom, garlic, and frozen peas into pan, cooking until onions are transclucent
  4. Remove vegetables from pan, and add butter and chicken stock cubes to hot pan. Mix well.
  5. Add noodles to colander, and dip in boiling water for 2-3 seconds, then toss into butter and chicken stock mixture in pan
  6. Remove noodles, and fry eggs in pan
  7. Add in noodles and vegetables, toss well
