Pasta Bake

Another classic, pasta bakes are easy to make and can be made in many different ways.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta (penne works well, or fusilli)
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 50g milk
  • 50g cheese
  • 600 ml milk
  • 3 cans tuna fish
  • 1 can sweetcorn

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions
  2. Melt butter into pan and add flour once melted, creating a roux
  3. Slowly add the milk and continously stir
  4. Once all milk is incorprated, add in half of cheese
  5. Stir in drained tuna and sweetcorn
  6. Add mixture to pasta in a baking dish
  7. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top
  8. Broil for 10 minutes on high
